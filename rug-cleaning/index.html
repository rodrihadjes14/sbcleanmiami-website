Rug Cleaning

Protect your investment with professional rug cleaning for area rugs, oriental rugs, wool, silk, and delicate fibers. Our eco-friendly process targets pet odor & stain removal while preserving color and pile. Optional pickup & delivery available in select areas.

Get a Free Quote

Why Choose Smart Brite for Rug Cleaning

We combine gentle detergents, fiber-safe techniques, and controlled drying to protect dyes and backing. Each rug is assessed for construction, fiber type, and prior cleanings to determine the safest and most effective method.

  • Fiber-safe methods for wool, cotton, silk, and synthetics
  • Pet odor & stain treatments (enzyme + deodorizing options)
  • Hand-detailing for fringes and sensitive areas
  • Transparent pricing with clear options before we start
  • Licensed & insured Miami-based team

Our Rug Cleaning Process

From basic wash to odor decontamination, we tailor the process to your rug’s needs. Our typical steps:

  1. Inspection & colorfastness testing
  2. Dry soil removal (dusting or thorough vacuuming)
  3. Fiber-appropriate wash and stain treatment
  4. Rinse and controlled drying to protect shape & dyes
  5. Post-inspection and grooming; protector available on request

Rug Cleaning FAQs

Do you clean wool and oriental rugs?

Yes. Our gentle, fiber-safe methods are designed for wool, silk, and hand-knotted oriental rugs, with colorfast testing before washing.

Can you remove pet odors and stains?

Yes. We use enzyme decontamination and deodorizing treatments, followed by controlled rinsing and drying to protect dyes and backing.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Pickup & delivery is available in select areas. Contact us with size and fiber details to confirm eligibility and timing.
