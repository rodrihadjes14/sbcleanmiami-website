Our Four Step Steam Cleaning Process

The steam cleaning process is structured for thorough soil removal and fiber care. Each step is explained so you understand how your carpets are treated.

  1. Eco friendly pre spraying – Preconditioning with biodegradable solutions loosens soil and oils embedded in the pile.
  2. Scrubbing – Agitation distributes the solution and lifts fibers to improve cleaning coverage.
  3. Spot treatment – Specific stains and high traffic lanes are treated individually with fiber safe agents.
  4. Hot steam extraction – Hot water extraction rinses the carpet and recovers suspended soil, leaving a balanced and fresh surface.

Deep and Steam Carpet Cleaning

Deep carpet cleaning Aventura and steam carpet cleaning Aventura are suitable for areas with visible soil, pet contamination, and long term use. This method extracts contaminants that vacuums and surface cleaners cannot reach. Drying generally occurs within six to twelve hours depending on carpet type and room airflow. Walking on damp carpet with clean socks or covers is acceptable, but limit traffic until fully dry to prevent re soiling.
