Pembroke Pines Carpet Cleaning — FAQs

How soon can I book?

Most appointments are available within 24–48 hours, with same-week carpet cleaning in Pembroke Pines during off-peak times.

Do you remove pet odors and stains?

Yes. We use enzyme treatments for pet urine and odor removal, followed by hot water extraction and a neutralizing rinse for a fresh finish.

Is steam cleaning safe for my carpet?

Yes. Our steam carpet cleaning method is fiber-safe. We test and adjust heat, pressure, and passes to match your carpet type.

How long does drying take?

Typical dry times are 3–6 hours depending on ventilation, humidity, and pile thickness. High-CFM extraction and airflow speed drying.
