Gentle cleaning for area rugs, oriental rugs, wool, silk, and synthetics—condo-friendly access and careful handling.

Area Rug Cleaning Aventura — Why Residents Choose Us

Our Aventura team uses fiber-safe detergents, controlled rinsing, and careful drying to protect dyes, pile, and backing. Expect transparent pricing and a satisfaction-first approach from a licensed & insured local provider.

  • Fiber-safe methods for wool, silk, cotton, and synthetics
  • Pet odor & stain removal with enzyme and deodorizing options
  • Hand-detailing for fringes and delicate areas
  • Condo-friendly equipment for elevators and tight access

Oriental Rug Cleaning in Aventura — Expert Care

Hand-knotted and dyed rugs receive pre-clean colorfast tests and construction checks. We tailor chemistry and dwell times by fiber type, then use controlled airflow for shape retention and sheen.

Our Rug Cleaning Process

  1. Inspection & colorfastness testing
  2. Dry soil removal (thorough vacuuming or dusting)
  3. Fiber-appropriate wash and targeted stain treatment
  4. Rinse & controlled drying to protect dyes and backing
  5. Post-inspection, grooming; protector available on request
