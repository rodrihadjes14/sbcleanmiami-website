FAQs

How long does carpet or upholstery take to dry?

Most carpets and sofas dry in 2–6 hours depending on fiber type, ventilation, and humidity. We use low-residue chemistry to speed drying.

Are your cleaning products eco-friendly and safe for pets?

Yes. We select eco-friendly solutions and always pre-test fabrics. Enzyme options are available for pet odor removal.

Do you service condos and high-rises in Miami and Miami Beach?

Yes. Our setup is condo-friendly—we follow building access rules and use elevator-safe equipment.

Can you remove tough stains?

We perform targeted stain treatment during cleaning. While results depend on fiber and stain age, we’ll set expectations upfront.
