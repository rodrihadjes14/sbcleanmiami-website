Upholstery Cleaning FAQs

How often should I clean my upholstery?

Every 12–18 months for regular use; sooner with pets or allergies.

Can you clean microfiber, linen, or leather?

We clean most fabrics. Leather is cleaned and conditioned by hand when appropriate.

Do you remove pet odors from couches?

Yes, we use targeted enzyme treatments for many pet odors and stains.

How long until I can sit on it again?

Typical dry time is 2–6 hours depending on ventilation and humidity.
