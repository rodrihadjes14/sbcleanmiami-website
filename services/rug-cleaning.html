Rug Cleaning FAQs

Do you clean oriental and wool rugs?

Yes. We assess fiber and dyes and use methods appropriate for delicate rugs.

Can pet odors be removed from rugs?

We apply targeted enzyme treatments; results depend on rug material and severity.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

For certain rugs and jobs we can arrange pickup—ask for availability in your area.

How long does a rug take to dry?

Typically 4–24 hours depending on thickness, ventilation, and method used.
