Air Duct Cleaning FAQs

How often should I clean my air ducts?

Typically every 3–5 years, or sooner after renovations, with pets, or if you notice dust buildup.

Will this help with dust and allergies?

Cleaning can reduce dust and some allergens. Results vary by home and HVAC use.

How long does the service take?

Most single-family homes take 1–3 hours; larger systems may take longer.

Do you offer sanitizing treatments?

Yes, optional treatments are available when appropriate and used as directed.
