Rug Cleaning in Miami, FL

Gentle cleaning for area rugs, wool, and oriental rugs. Pet odor removal and targeted stain treatments with fiber-safe methods.

Get a Free Quote

Area Rug Cleaning Miami — Why Homeowners Choose Us

Our Miami team uses low-residue detergents, controlled rinsing, and careful drying to protect color, pile, and backing. Expect clear communication, transparent pricing, and meticulous workmanship from a licensed & insured local provider.

  • Fiber-safe process for wool, cotton, silk, and synthetics
  • Pet odor & stain removal with enzyme decontamination
  • Hand-detailing for fringes and delicate areas
  • Optional pickup available in select neighborhoods

Oriental Rug Cleaning in Miami — Expert Care

Hand-knotted and dyed rugs receive pre-clean tests for colorfastness and construction. We select chemistry and dwell times based on fiber type, then use careful rinsing and controlled airflow to preserve shape and sheen.

Our Rug Cleaning Process

  1. Inspection & colorfastness testing
  2. Dry soil removal (thorough vacuuming or dusting)
  3. Fiber-appropriate wash and stain treatment
  4. Rinse & controlled drying to protect dyes and backing
  5. Post-inspection, grooming, and protector on request

Miami Rug Cleaning — FAQs

Do you pick up rugs in Miami?

Pickup may be available depending on rug size and location. Tell us the fiber and dimensions to confirm availability and timing.

Is your process safe for wool or silk?

Yes. Our wool & silk rug cleaning methods are designed for delicate fibers, starting with colorfast testing and fiber-safe chemistry.

Can you remove pet odors and stains?

Yes. We apply enzyme decontamination and deodorizing treatments for pet odor removal, followed by controlled rinsing and drying.
