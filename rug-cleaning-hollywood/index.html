Why Hollywood Chooses Smart Brite

From the Boardwalk to Emerald Hills, homeowners trust our gentle detergents, controlled rinsing, and careful drying to preserve colors, pile, and backing. Expect transparent pricing and licensed, insured service.

  • Fiber-safe methods for wool, silk, cotton, and synthetics
  • Pet odor & stain removal with enzyme and deodorizing options
  • Hand-detailing for fringes and delicate areas
  • Transparent pricing—clear options before we start
  • Condo-friendly equipment for elevators and tight access

Area Rug Cleaning — Gentle, Thorough Care

Every rug is inspected for construction and fiber type. We choose chemistry and dwell times based on colorfast tests, then rinse and groom for a soft, residue-low finish.

Oriental Rug Cleaning — Expert Handling

Hand-knotted pieces receive additional colorfast checks and low-agitation washes. Controlled airflow helps preserve shape and sheen for your oriental rugs.

Our Rug Cleaning Process

  1. Inspection & colorfastness testing
  2. Dry soil removal (vacuuming or dusting)
  3. Fiber-appropriate wash and targeted stain treatment
  4. Rinse & controlled drying to protect dyes and backing
  5. Final grooming; protector available on request
