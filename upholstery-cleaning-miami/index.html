Upholstery Cleaning in Miami, FL

Professional sofa & couch cleaning for homes and condos in Miami. Fast drying, low-residue chemistry, and pet odor & stain removal for fabric and microfiber sectionals, chairs, and more.

Get a Free Quote

Why Miami Chooses Smart Brite

Miami-based, licensed & insured technicians. We pair eco-friendly detergents with controlled rinsing to leave upholstery soft—not sticky—and ready to use sooner.

  • Fast-dry upholstery cleaning (typically 2–6 hours)
  • Targeted stain treatment for spills and traffic marks
  • Pet odor removal with enzyme/deodorizing options
  • Condo-friendly equipment and scheduling
  • Transparent pricing with clear options

Sofa, Couch & Sectional Cleaning

From loveseats to large sectionals, we pre-test fabrics, choose the safest method (steam or low-moisture), and finish with a neutralizing rinse for a soft feel.

Fabrics We Service

Microfiber, polyester, cotton blends, linen, rayon, and many synthetics. We pre-test unknown fabrics and advise when dry-solvent or low-moisture methods are best.

Our Upholstery Cleaning Process

  1. Inspection, fabric ID & colorfast test
  2. Vacuuming and pre-treatment of soils
  3. Fabric-appropriate steam/low-moisture cleaning
  4. Neutralizing rinse for a soft, residue-low finish
  5. Grooming & airflow assistance to speed drying
  6. Protector application available on request
