Miami Carpet Cleaning — FAQs

How soon can I book?

Most appointments are available within 24–48 hours, with same-week carpet cleaning in Miami during non-peak times.

Do you remove pet odors and stains?

Yes. We use enzyme treatments for pet urine and odor removal, followed by hot water extraction and a neutralizing rinse for lasting freshness.

Is steam cleaning safe for my carpet?

Yes. Our controlled steam carpet cleaning process is fiber-safe. We test areas first and tailor heat, pressure, and passes to your carpet type.

How long does drying take?

Typical dry times are 3–6 hours depending on ventilation, humidity, and pile density. We use high-CFM extraction and air movement to speed it up.

Do you service condos and high-rises?

Yes. We regularly service Brickell and Downtown high-rises. Our compact equipment and scheduling make access and parking simple.
