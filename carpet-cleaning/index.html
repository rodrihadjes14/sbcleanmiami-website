Carpet Cleaning FAQs

How long does carpet take to dry?

Most carpets dry in 3–6 hours depending on ventilation, humidity, and pile density. We use high-CFM extraction and airflow to accelerate drying.

Are your products safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use eco-friendly detergents and a neutralizing rinse to leave a low-residue, family-friendly finish.

Do you remove pet odors and stains?

Yes. Enzyme treatments target urine crystals and odor sources, followed by hot water extraction for a fresh, neutral result.

Do you service condos and high-rises?

Absolutely. Our portable equipment and scheduling make access straightforward for high-rises across Miami, Miami Beach, Aventura, and Sunny Isles.
