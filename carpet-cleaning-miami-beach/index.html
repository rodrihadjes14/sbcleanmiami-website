Our Four Step Steam Cleaning Process

The process is designed to remove soil efficiently while keeping the fiber balanced and comfortable underfoot.

  1. Eco friendly pre spraying – Preconditioning loosens tracked oils and particulate soil so it can be rinsed away.
  2. Scrubbing – Light agitation distributes the solution and lifts matted yarns without over wetting.
  3. Spot treatment – Specific stains and traffic lanes receive focused attention with fiber appropriate agents.
  4. Hot steam extraction – Hot water extraction rinses and recovers soil for a clean, neutral finish and a fresher feel.

Deep and Steam Carpet Cleaning

Deep carpet cleaning Miami Beach and steam carpet cleaning Miami Beach help restore appearance in living rooms, bedrooms, corridors, and lobby areas. Dry times vary with airflow and humidity.
